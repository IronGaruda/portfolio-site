GoFile's utilizes a very functional and user-oriented design for their main page. All of the most important information and available site features are accessible directly from the main page or side menu.

Gofile's menu is integrated directly into the main page of the site as a togglable sidebar. This menu houses every service the site offers; including file sharing, accessing their custom API, donating to or contacting the founder, checking their FAQ or by congregating online coupons.

This service has a very unique method of logging in. Rather than registering an account with an email and password and then logging into that account, GoFile has you enter your e-mail address which then sends you a one-time use code. This code is then entered instead of a password and allows you to securely log in and manage your account.
